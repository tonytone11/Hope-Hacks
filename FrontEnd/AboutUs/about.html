ABOUT

Our Goal

At open arms immigration, our goal is to create a supportive and accessible platform for immigrants seeking assistance with food resources. We aim to connect individuals and families with local food banks and community services, ensuring no one goes hungry while adjusting to life in a new environment.

We are committed to:

  • Empowering immigrants by providing accurate and up-to-date information on nearby food banks.
  • Breaking down language and cultural barriers to make accessing resources easier.
  • Building a sense of community and support for those in need.
